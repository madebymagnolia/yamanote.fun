Station Melodies

Every station on Tokyo's Yamanote Line plays its own short departure melody — a hassha jingle that signals the doors are about to close. This app collects all of them and lets you ride the loop like a playlist, inner or outer, station by station.

I built it as a love letter to those few seconds of sound that bookend every journey. Swipe (or use the controls) to move between stations and let the loop run on its own.
